1. Overview

NIRAS AI (“the Application,” “we,” “our,” or “us”) is an AI-powered exam authoring and educational content creation platform developed by NIR Academy, operated by Dr. Niki Majidi. NIRAS AI enables educators and teachers to create, manage, and export professional exams, questions, worksheets, and educational materials.

This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use our web and Android application. By using NIRAS AI, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Key commitments: We do not sell your personal data. We do not use your content for advertising. We do not share your data with third parties except as described in this policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email address — used for authentication and account identification
  • Password — stored in encrypted form via Supabase Auth; never stored in plaintext
  • Display name — optional, provided by the user in profile settings

2.2 User-Generated Educational Content

  • Exams, sections, and metadata (title, subject, CEFR level, time limit, scoring)
  • Questions, answer choices, correct answers, and explanations
  • Reading passages, writing prompts, and speaking tasks
  • Text content submitted for AI-assisted question generation
  • Uploaded documents or files used as source material for AI generation

2.3 Profile and Preference Data

  • Profile name and optional avatar image (stored locally on device only, not uploaded to our servers)
  • Application preferences and settings

2.4 Technical and Usage Data

  • Device type, operating system, and browser version
  • Internet Protocol (IP) address
  • Pages accessed and features used within the Application
  • Error logs and performance diagnostics
  • Session duration and access timestamps

Technical data is collected by our infrastructure providers (Vercel, Supabase) as part of standard operations.

3. How We Use Information

  • Authentication and account management — to verify identity and provide secure access
  • Core functionality — to enable exam creation, editing, saving, and PDF export
  • AI content generation — to process your input and generate educational content via Groq API
  • Service improvement — to monitor performance, identify errors, and improve stability
  • User support — to respond to inquiries and resolve issues
  • Legal compliance — to comply with applicable laws and regulations

We do not use your personal data or educational content for advertising, profiling, behavioral tracking, or any purpose beyond providing the core service described above.

4. AI Processing and Third-Party AI Services

NIRAS AI uses the Groq API (api.groq.com) to power AI-assisted question generation features.

  • Text content you submit is transmitted to Groq servers for processing
  • Groq processes this content solely to generate the requested educational output
  • AI requests are routed through our secure server-side proxy — API credentials are never exposed client-side
  • We do not use AI input or output for model training, advertising, or profiling
  • All AI API requests are transmitted over encrypted HTTPS/TLS connections

Groq's privacy practices are governed by the Groq Privacy Policy.

5. Data Storage and Retention

5.1 Where Data is Stored

  • User accounts and exam content are stored in Supabase cloud database infrastructure, located in the United States
  • The Application is hosted on Vercel infrastructure, distributed globally via CDN
  • Profile avatar images are stored locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers

5.2 Retention Period

  • Account data and content are retained for as long as your account remains active
  • Upon account deletion, personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized or aggregated technical logs may be retained longer for security and infrastructure purposes
  • Data required for legal or compliance obligations will be retained only for the period required by law

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party.

6.1 Service Providers

  • Supabase — receives account credentials, authentication tokens, and exam content for database storage and authentication
  • Groq — receives text content submitted for AI generation, used only to produce the requested output
  • Vercel — receives standard web request data (IP address, browser headers) as part of hosting

6.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your data if required by applicable law, regulation, legal process, or governmental authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of NIR Academy, our users, or the public.

6.3 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale, user data may be transferred. Users will be notified of any such change via an updated policy or direct communication.

7. User Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights:

  • Right of Access — Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Right of Rectification — Update or correct inaccurate data via account settings or by contacting us
  • Right of Erasure — Request permanent deletion of your account and personal data (see Section 8)
  • Right to Restrict Processing — Request that we limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability — Request an export of your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Right to Object — Object to certain processing of your personal data

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at info@niracademy.com. We will respond to all legitimate requests within 30 days.

8. Account Deletion and Data Deletion

You have the right to delete your account and all associated personal data at any time.

All deletion requests are processed within 30 days of submission.

8.1 How to Request Account Deletion

  • Email request: Send an email to info@niracademy.com with subject “Account Deletion Request” and the email address associated with your account
  • In-app request: Contact us via the in-app support option if available

8.2 What Data Is Deleted

Upon a verified deletion request, the following data will be permanently deleted within 30 days:

  • Your account credentials (email address and authentication data)
  • All exams, questions, and educational content you have created
  • Your profile information and display name
  • All records associating your identity with your content

8.3 Data That May Be Retained

  • Anonymized or aggregated usage statistics that cannot be linked to your identity
  • Technical logs retained by infrastructure providers for security and compliance, subject to their own policies
  • Records required for compliance with applicable legal, tax, or financial regulations

8.4 Confirmation of Deletion

You will receive email confirmation once your account and data have been deleted. If you do not receive confirmation within 30 days, please contact info@niracademy.com.

9. Security Measures

  • Encryption in transit: All data is transmitted using HTTPS/TLS encryption
  • Encryption at rest: User data stored in Supabase is protected using industry-standard encryption
  • Secure authentication: Authentication is managed by Supabase Auth with secure token-based access controls
  • Server-side API security: AI API keys are stored as server-side environment variables and never exposed client-side
  • Access controls: Row-level security in Supabase ensures users can only access their own data
  • Password protection: Passwords are hashed and salted; we never store plaintext passwords

While we implement reasonable security measures, no system is completely secure. We encourage users to use strong, unique passwords and to notify us immediately if they suspect unauthorized access.

10. Children’s Privacy

NIRAS AI is designed for educational professionals and is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the applicable age of digital consent in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal data to us without your consent, please contact us at info@niracademy.com and we will promptly delete such information.

11. International Data Transfers

Our infrastructure providers — Supabase and Vercel — store and process data primarily in the United States. If you access the Application from outside the United States, including from the European Economic Area (EEA), United Kingdom, or Turkey, your personal data may be transferred to and processed in a country with different data protection standards.

We take appropriate measures to ensure international data transfers comply with applicable laws, including through reliance on our service providers’ compliance frameworks and standard contractual clauses where applicable.
