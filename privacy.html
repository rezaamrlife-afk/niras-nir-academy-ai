Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

NIRA Academy AI is an AI-powered educational platform designed to help teachers create exams, prompts, and learning materials. This policy explains how we handle your data.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Email address (when you sign in or register)
  • User-generated content (prompts, generated exam content, saved materials)
  • Basic usage data (app interactions, feature usage)

2. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • Provide and improve AI-generated educational services
  • Store your content and prompt history
  • Improve user experience and app performance

3. Data Storage

We use Supabase to securely store user data. Data is stored on encrypted servers and accessed only as necessary to provide the service.

4. AI Processing

This app uses third-party AI APIs (including Groq and similar providers) to generate content based on your input. Prompts you submit may be processed by these providers in accordance with their own privacy policies.

5. Data Sharing

  • We do not sell your personal data.
  • We only share data with service providers necessary to operate the application (Supabase, Groq).
  • We do not share your data with advertisers or third-party marketers.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable technical measures to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure. We encourage you to use a strong password and keep your account credentials confidential.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request access to the data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting your account

To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for use by educators and adults. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.
